How we think.

Most product teams ship software. We ship taste. Our work is opinionated, careful, and built to outlast the trend cycle.

We believe in small senior teams, slow research, fast iteration, and the kind of attention that turns a tool into an experience.

We design with engineers and engineer with designers — that is the studio. Half our team writes code, half our team writes pixels. The boundary dissolves somewhere between Figma and Xcode.

Craft is a discipline, not an aesthetic. We treat typography, motion, accessibility, performance, and naming with the same care. The result is software that feels inevitable — that earns its place on the home screen.
