We are Resn. Hello.

A small, sharp team of designers, engineers, animators and strategists working out of a studio on the Wellington waterfront. We've spent two decades shipping category-defining work for brands like Air New Zealand, Google, BBC, Lego, and the All Blacks.

We don't believe in safe. We believe in well-considered risk, in craft, and in the kind of partnership that turns ambitious briefs into enduring work.
