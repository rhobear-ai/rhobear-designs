This is how we do it

Dogstudio is a design & technology firm working globally from our offices based in Belgium and Chicago.

Our strong focus on producing high quality & emotional brandings, digital products and experiences has led us to work with prestigious brands, world-famous institutions, festivals, museums and entertainment companies.

We're best known for our work with Tomorrowland, Navy Pier, MSI Chicago, the Kennedy Center, and the Royal Opera of Wallonia — each project built on craft, curiosity, and a refusal to do work that doesn't earn its place.
