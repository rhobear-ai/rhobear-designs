A global leader in groundbreaking digital design and strategy.

Founded in Paris in 2014, Immersive Garden has grown into a 60-strong studio of designers, engineers, and creative technologists working at the intersection of art, design, and code.

We are best known for work with luxury Maisons, cultural institutions, and ambitious challengers — for whom we build immersive web experiences, brand identities, and editorial platforms.

Our work has been recognized by Awwwards, FWA, CSS Design Awards, and the Webby Awards.
