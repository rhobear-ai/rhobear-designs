Studio philosophy.

Exo Ape is a digital creative studio of 52 people in Amsterdam, working at the intersection of design, engineering, and brand. We were founded on a simple idea: that the best digital work happens when designers and engineers sit at the same table and refuse to ship anything they don't believe in.

We work in small senior teams over long engagements — typically four to twelve months. No middle management, no account theatre, no pitch-and-run. Just designers, engineers, and a small handful of producers making things we're proud of.

The work has been recognized by FWA, Awwwards, Webby, ADC, D&AD, Brand New, and the Lovie Awards — but the metric we care about most is whether the people we work with come back.
