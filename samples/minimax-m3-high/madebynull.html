The Studio

Null is a digital experience studio designing spaces for fun, commerce and conversation on the web.

We are a nimble, craft-focused multidisciplinary team working out of Europe and Western Africa. We create delightful and compelling web experiences for clients and friends alike.

Over the last four years, our work has garnered shouts from industry tastemakers such as the Webbys, Awwwards, Klikkentheke, and Commarts Magazine.

Our Ethos

Inspired by the capacity of the web to provide shared experiences on a large scale and drive change, we treat every project as an opportunity to make something meaningful.

Although the web is our preferred medium, we do not have a preferred style or signature; we simply create vivid and fun experiences.

Focus

Art Direction · Digital Strategy · Website Design · Interaction Design · Motion Design · Content Management Systems · Creative Development (WebGL / ThreeJS) · Shopify

The Team

Null is headed by partners Isaac and Seyi, collaborating on strategy and leading design and development respectively. The partners collaborate with a trusted roster of designers, illustrators, animators, engineers and producers for each project.
